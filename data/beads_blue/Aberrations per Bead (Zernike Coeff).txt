Aberrations per Bead (Zernike Coefficient Plot)
Code section:

figure
plot(1:numzers,zernike_coeffs,'k-*','MarkerSize',5)

What you see:
A line plot showing the fitted Zernike aberration coefficients for all beads.
The x-axis labels the Zernike modes (n,m), and the y-axis shows the coefficients (in units of mλ).
