Mean and Standard Deviation of Aberrations
Code section:

figure
errorbar(1:numzers,mean_aber,std_aber,'Color','black','LineWidth',1)
plot(1:numzers,mean_aber,'k-*','MarkerSize',10)


What you see:
A line plot with error bars showing the mean and standard deviation of each Zernike aberration mode across all beads.
This quantifies the typical aberration and its variability in your sample.
