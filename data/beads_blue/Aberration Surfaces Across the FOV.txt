Aberration Surfaces Across the FOV (Subplots)
Code section:

figure('units','normalized','outerposition',[0 0 1 1])
for izer = 1:size(params.aberrations,1)
    hsub = subplot(2,3,izer);
    surf(Xq,Yq,Vq,'FaceAlpha',0.7)
    plot3(X,Y,V,"o",'LineWidth',3,'MarkerSize',10)
    ...
end

What you see:
A figure with multiple subplots (typically 6, one for each Zernike mode you fit), each showing a 3D surface plot of a particular aberration mode across the FOV.
The surface represents the spatial variation of that aberration, and dots indicate bead positions.
