Best Practice for Thesis Figures

Show both bypass and split mode for each excitation:
This lets the reader see the change in aberrations introduced by the splitter.

If space/time limited, prioritize split mode:
If your thesis is focused on splitter characterization, split mode results are most critical.

Include bypass mode for completeness:
It’s valuable for showing that any aberrations in split mode are truly due to the splitter, not the microscope itself.

How to Structure Your Thesis Section
Compare bypass mode (system baseline) to split mode (splitter engaged) for each wavelength.
Discuss differences and implications for imaging quality and multi-color experiments.
If possible, also show simultaneous excitation in split mode for registration analysis.
